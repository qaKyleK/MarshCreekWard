Cleaning Instructions

    Bathrooms:

  • Sweep and if needed, mop the floors.
  • Clean toilets, sinks and mirrors.
  • Restock toilet paper, paper towels, changing table covers, soap, air deodorizers.
  • Collect trash and line trash cans; Dispose of trash in the dumpster in the parking lot.

    • Kitchen & sidewalks:

  • Sweep and mop floors.
  • Clean counters and sink.
  • Wash, dry and put away any dishes left out.
  • Clean refrigerators; Throw away any unlabeled food.
  • Collect trash and line trash cans; Dispose of trash in the dumpster in the parking lot.
  • Pick up trash along sidewalks that face the front of the building as well as the back of the building. Also pick up debris in the parking lots.

    • Classrooms & hallways:

  • Clean blackboards / dry erase boards.
  • Dust as needed.
  • Clean any glass on the doors and disinfect door handles.
  • Sweep or vacuum the floors.
  • Collect trash and line trash cans; Dispose of trash in the dumpster in the parking lot.

    • Chapel & entrances:

  • Vacuum floors.
  • Dust and vacuum pews as needed.
  • Collect trash and line trash cans; Dispose of trash in the dumpster in the parking lot.
  • Sweep or vacuum entrance lobby floors.
  • Clean all glass doors in the entrance lobby (front and rear); disinfect door handles.
